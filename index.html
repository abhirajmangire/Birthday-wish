A Letter to Dad

Happy Birthday, Pappa ❤️
I don't say this often, but I want you to know how much you mean to me. When I think of you, I think of a hardworking man who has always done everything he could for his family. You have always been strict when needed, but behind that strictness was care, love, and a desire to see me become a better person. One thing I admire most about you is the trust you have always shown in me. You never forced your choices on me. Instead, you gave me the freedom to make my own decisions, learn from my mistakes, and find my own path in life. That trust means more to me than I can ever explain. You have always supported me, even when things were uncertain, and your belief in me has given me the confidence to keep moving forward. Many of the opportunities and dreams I have today are possible because of the sacrifices, hard work, and support you have given me throughout my life. There is one thing I probably don't say enough: I love you very much, Pappa. More than words can express. I am proud to be your son, and I feel lucky to have you as my father. On your birthday, I just want to thank you for everything you have done and continue to do for our family. I hope this year brings you the happiness, peace, and success that you truly deserve.
Happy Birthday, Pappa. ❤️
Love always,
Your Son

— Your Son, Abhiraj ❤️
